sweet potato scramble

eggs and stuff in a pan. boom.

ingredients

  • eggs
  • sweet potato
  • coconut oil
  • salt
  • probably butter too
  • literally any other vegetable

steps

  1. heat pan to medium-ish temp with coconut oil
  2. chop up sweet potato into smallish chunks and add to pan
  3. salt dat shiz a little more than you think you should
  4. reduce heat to medium-low-ish temp
  5. add literally any other vege (onion and shiitakes strongly recommended)
  6. maybe a little more salt?
  7. probably add butter
  8. other spices if you want
  9. once sweet potato is soft, drop 2 to 6 eggs (go big plz)
  10. scramble (see below for pro egg technique)
  11. plate dat shiz and add sauce of choice (mayo is lit)

egg technique

  1. allow whites to cook for about 30 to 45 seconds before popping yolks
  2. pop dem yolks and fold into ingredients until it all swirly but don't over-stir plz
  3. allow bottom to cook a bit
  4. slice into quarters with spatula and flip each chunk only once
  5. el fin
